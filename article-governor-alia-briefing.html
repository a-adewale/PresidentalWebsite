State House Briefing: Governor Hyacinth Alia Addresses Journalists

July 8, 2026

Governor Hyacinth Alia Addresses Journalists at the State House

Governor Hyacinth Alia addressed journalists at the State House following official engagements at the Presidential Villa, Abuja.

The briefing focused on development priorities, security cooperation and the importance of federal-state collaboration in addressing public needs.

The State House Press Office said regular briefings remain important for communicating government activities and keeping citizens informed.
